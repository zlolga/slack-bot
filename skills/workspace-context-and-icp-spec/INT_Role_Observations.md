## Source priority by tier

**Tier 0 (baseline):**
- LinkedIn search for titles within customer-logo companies.
- Customer-side case studies — who's named as the buyer / champion.
- Job ads from customer-logo companies — reveals reporting lines, tech stack, KPIs.
- G2/Capterra reviewer profiles — title + company.
- Press releases — who's quoted from customer side.
- Conference speaker lists at industry events the client attends.

**Tier 1:** founder LinkedIn comment/reply threads (who they engage with on customer side); LinkedIn post engagement on customer-themed posts.

**Tier 2:** client CRM exports, sales interviews on real won/lost deals.

Tier 0 alone gets us solid role hypotheses, plus 2–3 example real people per role.
