*Stage 2. Facts only. Every line cites a URL.*

Separate verifiable facts from positioning. No source = not a fact = goes to Open Questions.

---

## Source priority by tier

**Tier 0 (baseline):** website (Product, Pricing, Customers, Tech/Specs pages), company LinkedIn page, Crunchbase, news/press, G2/Capterra, SEC filings if relevant, headcount on LinkedIn.

**Tier 1 (reinforces Tier 0):** company LinkedIn posts announcing customers / features / milestones; founder posts mentioning real numbers; podcast/interview mentions of specs and pricing.

**Tier 2 (highest accuracy):** internal decks, demo videos, case studies, CRM exports.

The bulk of this file is fillable from Tier 0 alone.
